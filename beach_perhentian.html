Perhentian Island

Perhentian Island, Terengganu

About

The Perhentian Islands offer tourist-free tropical beaches, spectacular snorkelling conditions and affordable scuba diving schools. While the tourist trail might lead you to Penang or Langkawi, these islands are surprisingly uncommercial.

What can we do?

Scuba Diving

One of the most popular things to do Perhentian Islands has to offer, diving in the Perhentians allows you to enjoy the coral reefs around the islands and it's also easy to organise diving trips further afield to Redang Island or Pulau Lang Tengah.

Jungle Trekking

If you love the thrill of a great adventure, these tropical islands offer a lot of interesting trekking opportunities as well. Discover lush foliage and interesting wildlife as you hike along woodlands.

Sea Kayaking

Kayaking Perhentian Islands is a great way to see more of the islands and get some great views from the water. You may even be able to find your own beach! Outside the monsoon, the waters are usually calm and you can go alone or with a tour.

Nearby food

Bayu Restaurant


Delight in a delectable selection of local and international cuisine prepared using fresh, local and seasonal ingredients. A blend of mouth-watering Western and Malaysian favourites tempts your tastebuds with a rich variety of flavours.

Ewan's Café & Restaurant


A very homey place with nice home-cooked quality food. Easy to get to if you're on Coral Bay side, just walk through Ombak Dive Resort and you'll see it.

Belinda Café


Belinda Café is among our favourite restaurants in The Perhentian Islands for its Thai and Malay fusion food. The family-friendly spot on Perhentian Besar overlooks the beach and serves both breakfast and lunch. town.

8am - 9:30pm
