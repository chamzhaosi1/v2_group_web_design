About

In 1989, Genting Berhad and Genting Malaysia underwent a restructuring exercise, which resulted in Genting Malaysia acquiring from Genting Berhad its entire gaming, hotel and resort-related operations, inclusive of goodwill and other relevant assets.

  

What Can We Do?

Genting Skyworlds

This brand new Genting Skyworlds features 9 uniquely themed worlds spread over 26 acres of land. Explore a variety of world-class movie-themed rides and areas for all ages, from your little ones to teens, adults and the young at heart!

The Laughing Fish by Harry Ramsden

This Harry Ramsden is a very famous fast food restaurant in the UK, and currently the only branch in Malaysia is in Genting!

The restaurant cum pub offers a casual dining atmosphere to enjoy their signature North Atlantic fish coated in crispy, fresh batter still made from Harry's own secret recipe.
